B.K.S. Iyengar

Who is B.K.S. Iyengar?

B.K.S. Iyengar is one of the most influential yoga teachers of all kind. Dubbed such names as "the Michelangelo of Yoga” and the “king of yogis,” Iyengar was credited for bringing yoga to the West (Iyengar Yoga National Association of the U.S, para 1, 2017).
